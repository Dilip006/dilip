About Nordicora

Nordicora is dedicated to bridging the vibrant cultures of Asia and Europe through trade, trust, and tradition. Our journey is a story of connection, quality, and cultural exchange.

2005 - The Journey Begins

Nordicora was founded with a vision to connect Asia and Europe through the trade of premium spices, starting with Ceylon cinnamon from Sri Lanka.

2010 - Expanding Horizons

We broadened our offerings to include luxury goods, bringing European sophistication to Asian markets and vice versa.

2015 - Cultural Bridges

Nordicora launched initiatives to promote cultural exchange, supporting artisans and preserving heritage crafts across continents.

2020 - A Global Network

Our network grew to span over 20 countries, ensuring seamless trade and fostering partnerships built on trust and quality.

2025 - Today and Beyond

Nordicora continues to innovate, bringing the best of Asia and Europe to the world while staying true to our roots of authenticity and excellence.
